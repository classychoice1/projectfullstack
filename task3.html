Roasted chicken

list of ingredients for preparing Roasted chicken

  • 1 meduim whole chicken
  • Butter
  • salt
  • pepper
  • onions
  • breadcrumbs

steps to prepare roasted chicken

  1. preheat the oven
  2. melt the butter and onions, cook for about 10 minutes.
  3. Add your breadcrubs and seasoning to the chicken
  4. cook the chicken in the oven for about 25 minutes
  5. place the chicken in the roasting tin and spread with butter,season with salt and pepper.
  6. After 15 minutes your roasted chicken is ready.
